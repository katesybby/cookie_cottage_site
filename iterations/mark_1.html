Our Story

Handmade on purpose.

No corporate formulas. No frozen dough trucks. Just a mom, a mixer, and a ridiculous amount of butter.

Why your cookie tastes different here

Jen, the owner and baker, starts at 4:00am in Rigby making dough, frosting, and rotating through up to 150 flavors she’s developed over years. Every cookie is hand-scooped and frosted in small batches.

  • • Real ingredients – no shortening, no weird preservatives.
  • • Short shelf life on purpose – cookies are kept 2–3 days max.
  • • Small team – about three core employees keep quality insane.
  • • Local first – Rigby roots, now bringing the chaos to Rexburg.
